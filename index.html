Diablo Valley Villages

Welcome to Diablo Valley Villages

Diablo Valley Villages (DVV) is a 501c3 nonprofit network of local Villages dedicated to helping older adults live independently, safely, and with meaningful connections in the communities they love.

Through a combination of volunteer support, social engagement, and healthy aging programs, we empower adults to remain active and connected while navigating the opportunities and challenges of aging.

Our Villages are rooted in neighbor-to-neighbor support — members helping members — strengthened by a shared regional infrastructure that provides professional coordination, technology, and resources. Whether someone needs a ride to an appointment, help with technology, a friendly visit, or opportunities to learn and socialize, Diablo Valley Villages is here to help.

Each affiliate Village serves its local community while benefiting from the strength of our collaborative network.

Our Local Villages

Lamorinda Village (LV)

Lamorinda Village, founded in 2016, serves Lafayette, Moraga, and Orinda. As the founding Village in the Diablo Valley network, LV has built a vibrant community of members and volunteers who support one another through transportation, home assistance, technology help, social gatherings, and a wide range of healthy aging programs including fitness, balance, brain enrichment, and educational workshops. Lamorinda Village is known for its welcoming culture, strong partnerships, and innovative programs that help older adults thrive while aging in place.

Clayton Valley Village (CVV)

Clayton Valley Village serves the Clayton and Concord area, bringing neighbors together to create a supportive and connected community for older adults. CVV focuses on building local relationships through volunteer support, social opportunities, and programs that promote independence and well-being. Members enjoy practical help when needed along with opportunities to stay engaged and connected close to home.

San Ramon Valley Village

San Ramon Valley Village serves Danville, San Ramon, and surrounding communities, supporting older adults who want to remain active, independent, and socially connected. SRVV offers volunteer services, educational programs, and social events designed to promote healthy aging and community engagement. The Village provides a welcoming environment where neighbors support one another through life’s transitions.
